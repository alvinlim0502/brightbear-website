About Bright Bear

A neighbourhood preschool with a loving team, modern classrooms, and a curriculum that grows with your child from playful discovery to Primary 1 readiness.

Ages 3-4

Early Years (Playgroup)

Sensory play, music & movement, language exposure, and social skills in a cosy, safe environment.

Ages 5

Kindy Foundations

Thematic discovery projects, phonics, pre‑writing, Mandarin basics, and gentle maths.

Ages 6

Primary Prep

Stronger English, BM & Mandarin reading, problem‑solving maths, science starters, and study habits.

Programmes & Highlights

Balanced academics + joyful enrichment to build confidence.

Literacy & Numeracy

Systematic phonics, sight words, storytelling; hands‑on maths with concrete materials.

Music & Piano Time

Weekly piano lab and rhythm games to improve focus, memory, and fine motor skills.

Digital Corners

Teacher‑guided tablets and coding toys (no ads) to explore patterns, logic, and creativity.

Character & Life Skills

Kindness, independence, and teamwork through chores, role‑play, and class responsibilities.

Languages

English, Bahasa Malaysia, and Mandarin tracks with daily practice and songs.

Safe & Caring

CCTV at entrances, secure pickup, and regular parent updates via our app.

Why Families Choose Us

Similar clean structure you’re used to, but all fully branded to Bright Bear.

  • Structured path to Primary 1 readiness (MoE‑aligned)
  • Teacher:child ratio that ensures attention
  • Daily parent updates (meals, naps, photos)
  • Qualified educators trained in phonics & early numeracy
  • Affordable fees
